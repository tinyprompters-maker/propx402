PropX402 automatically surfaces costs derived from property age, construction era, weather exposure, and flood zone status — things that never appear on a listing sheet.

## Insurance Estimate

Base rate: **0.8% annual property value**, adjusted for:
- Age surcharge: up to +60% for 80+ year homes
- Tornado/hail risk: up to +40% (NOAA data)
- Flood zone: +$1,200/yr NFIP average if in flood zone

## Rehab Cost Estimate

Base cost per sqft by age (8–52 $/sqft) × condition multiplier (0.2–1.8x):

| Condition | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Excellent | 0.2x |
| Good | 0.5x |
| Average | 1.0x |
| Fair | 1.3x |
| Poor | 1.8x |

## Age-Based Risk Flags

| Risk | Trigger | Budget Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Lead paint | Built before 1978 | +$8K if Very High |
| Knob & tube wiring | Built before 1950 | $8–15K rewire |
| Asbestos | Built before 1980 | $2–8K testing/remediation |

<Note>
  **Example — 1411 8th Ave SE, Cedar Rapids IA (built 1910)**
  
  Insurance: $142/mo · Rehab mid: $86,010 · Lead paint: Very High · Wiring: likely knob & tube · Asbestos: possible. None of this appears on the listing.
</Note>
